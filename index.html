Alloxo Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 4, 2026

Alloxo ("the app") is a utility that helps you quickly open a WhatsApp chat with the number of a recent phone call. This policy explains how the app handles your information.

Data we access

With your permission, Alloxo reads your device call log to display your recent calls and detect when a call ends. Phone numbers are formatted on your device to open the corresponding WhatsApp chat.

Data collection and sharing

Alloxo does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device. The app has no internet access and no analytics or advertising SDKs. Call log information never leaves your device.

Data retention and deletion

A small list of recent calls is cached locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app removes all data it stored. You can also revoke the call log permission at any time in Android settings.

Children

The app is intended for users aged 18 and over.
